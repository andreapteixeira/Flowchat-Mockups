Access Slack

After completing all the previous lessons, you need to contact us to enable your White Label account from our end. We use Slack to provide support and keep in touch with you throughout the process. All major questions, answers, updates, and information are shared through this platform.

For extra support you can use Slack to access your own "Personal Private Channel", where you can chat directly with us about your account, and our "Pipeline Strategy Channel" were we go deep on Sales Strategy, suggest Messages copy, share Pipeline Templates, and explore techniques for identifying the perfect client profile.

Check your email for an invite to our FlowChat Workspace in Slack!

Step 1

Open FlowChat Channel on Slack. You received an invitation to FlowChat's Slack channel, by clicking on it you will open the Slack page and you should click "Continue With Email" and type your email address and your full name.

Step 2

Fill the verification code. After entering your email and name, a verification code will be sent to your email, so please go to your mailbox and copy that code to the page. After that you should have access to the FlowChat channel and your own private channel! Yeah!

Step 3

Tag Bruno in Your Private Channel. Now that you have access to your private channel, you should tag Bruno by using the "@Bruno Domingues" mention. This will notify Bruno that you need your white label domain to be enabled, and he will take care of it from there.

Step 4

White Label Browser Extension. To obtain your own extension through the White Label Plan, mention that in your private channel. We will then work on preparing and deploying your extension on the Chrome extension platform.

Step 5

Access Pipeline Strategy Channel. Now you also have access to FlowChat Workspace and to the Pipeline Strategy Channel, you can access it on Slack or just click the button below.

Open Pipeline Strategy Channel in Slack

In conclusion, enabling your White Label account is a simple process that requires you to contact us through Slack. By following the steps outlined in this tutorial, you can successfully get in touch with Bruno and have your white label domain enabled and your extension deployed.
